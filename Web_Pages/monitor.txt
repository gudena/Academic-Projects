<h1>monitor</h1>
<p>
A monitor or a display (also called screen or visual display unit) is an 
electronic visual display for computers. The monitor comprises the 
display device, circuitry and an enclosure. The display device in modern 
monitors is typically a thin film transistor liquid crystal display (TFT-LCD) thin panel, 
while older monitors use a cathode ray tube (CRT) about as deep as the screen size.
